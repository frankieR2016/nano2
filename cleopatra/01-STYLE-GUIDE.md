# Style Guide — The Last Pharaoh

## The 3-Line Style Block (paste verbatim into every prompt)

```
Art Style: Cinematic painterly realism with restrained brush texture, mature graphic novel composition
Color palette: lapis blue, Alexandrian gold, sand ochre, oxblood red, ivory white
Effects: warm Mediterranean light in Acts 1-2 shifting to cooler ashen palette as Rome closes in
```

## Palette Map
- Pages 1–9: warm — gold-dominant, lapis accents
- Pages 10–15: cooler — ash, oxblood, ivory dominant; gold only where Cleopatra performs

## Panel Rules
- 2 panels per page MAX (per nano2 rules)
- Pivotal pages (6, 10, 15) = single full-page panel, no borders
- Bold black panel borders, ~2-3% gutter

## Shot Type Reference
- Establishing: wide, architectural
- Drama: medium, faces visible
- Climax: full-page, no border

## Text Rules
- White speech bubbles, black outline, black text
- Captions: rectangular, top of panel, ivory background
- Max 10-12 words per bubble
- Silent panels MUST say "NO speech bubble. NO text."
