# Settings — The Last Pharaoh

## Alexandria
Marble palace on the harbor. The great Lighthouse (Pharos) on its island, visible from palace windows. Limestone colonnades, blue-tile floors, palm courtyards. Mediterranean sun. Smell of salt and incense. Color: gold + lapis.

## The Egyptian Desert (Eastern frontier)
Pale ochre dunes. Tents at dusk. Camels. Sky bruised pink-purple. Cold at night, blazing by day.

## Caesar's Apartment, Alexandria
Roman-style room inside the Egyptian palace. Oil lamps, scrolls, low couches, a single brazier. Tense, intimate.

## The Cydnus River, Tarsus
Slow brown river through reedy banks. Temples and Hellenistic architecture on shore. Crowds gathering. Late-afternoon honey light.

## Alexandria Streets at Night (Inimitable Livers)
Narrow lanes, hanging lamps, shuttered shops, drunken laughter, cats on walls. Warm orange pools of lamplight against deep blue night.

## The Bay of Actium (Greek coast)
Open water, hundreds of warships, smoke, dawn light. The end of a world.

## The Mausoleum, Alexandria
Half-built tomb. Marble blocks, scaffolding. Cool, echoing, dim. Final-act setting for pages 12-15.
